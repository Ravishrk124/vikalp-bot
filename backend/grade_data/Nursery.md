# Nursery Grade - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
The Nursery program at Vikalp Online School introduces children aged 3-4 years to the joy of learning through play-based activities. Our curriculum focuses on developing foundational skills in a nurturing, interactive online environment.

## Subjects
- **English**: Alphabet recognition, phonics introduction, simple words
- **Hindi**: Hindi varnamala (alphabet), basic sounds
- **Mathematics**: Numbers 1-20, shapes, colors, basic counting
- **EVS (Environmental Studies)**: My body, my family, animals, plants
- **Art & Craft**: Drawing, coloring, paper craft activities
- **Rhymes & Stories**: Nursery rhymes, storytelling sessions

## Syllabus Summary
- Recognition of English alphabets A-Z with phonics
- Recognition of Hindi alphabets
- Counting and writing numbers 1-20
- Identification of basic shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle)
- Primary and secondary colors
- Good habits and hygiene awareness
- Fine motor skills development through activities

## Teaching Method
- **Play-Based Learning**: Learning through games, songs, and interactive activities
- **Small Batch Sizes**: Maximum 8-10 students per class for personalized attention
- **Visual & Audio Content**: Colorful videos, animations, and songs
- **Parent Involvement**: Regular parent-teacher interactions and activity suggestions for home
- **Screen Time**: 30-45 minute sessions with breaks

## FAQs
**Q: What is the class duration for Nursery?**
A: Each class is 30-45 minutes with activity breaks.

**Q: How many days a week are classes held?**
A: Classes are held 5 days a week (Monday to Friday).

**Q: What technology is needed?**
A: A tablet or computer with internet connection and camera/microphone.

**Q: Is parent supervision required?**
A: Yes, we recommend parent presence during online classes for Nursery students.
