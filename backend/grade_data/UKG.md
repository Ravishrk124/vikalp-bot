# UKG (Upper Kindergarten) - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
UKG at Vikalp Online School prepares children aged 5-6 years for formal schooling. This bridge year focuses on school readiness with structured learning patterns.

## Subjects
- **English**: Reading simple sentences, writing, comprehension basics
- **Hindi**: Matras mastery, simple sentences, reading practice
- **Mathematics**: Numbers 1-100, addition & subtraction, word problems
- **EVS**: My neighborhood, transportation, plants & animals, weather
- **Art & Craft**: Creative projects, festival crafts
- **General Knowledge**: Current awareness, basic GK for kids

## Syllabus Summary
- Reading and writing simple sentences in English
- Hindi reading with all matras
- Numbers 1-100 with place value introduction
- Addition and subtraction up to 20
- Word problems with pictures
- Time concepts (morning, afternoon, evening)
- Money awareness (coins recognition)
- Safety rules and good manners
- Environmental awareness

## Teaching Method
- **Structured Classes**: 1-hour sessions with breaks
- **Reading Program**: Daily reading practice
- **Writing Skills**: Proper handwriting development
- **Concept Clarity**: Visual aids and manipulatives
- **Batch Size**: 12-15 students per class
- **Regular Assessments**: Monthly worksheets

## FAQs
**Q: How does UKG prepare for Grade 1?**
A: Focus on reading fluency, writing skills, and mathematical foundations.

**Q: What are class timings?**
A: Morning batch (9-10 AM) or Afternoon batch (3-4 PM).

**Q: Is there a second language option?**
A: Hindi is the second language; regional languages can be added on request.

**Q: How is screen time managed?**
A: 1-hour class with activity breaks every 20 minutes.
