# Grade 8 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 8 is the final year of middle school. Students aged 13-14 years prepare for high school with comprehensive curriculum coverage.

## Subjects
- **English**: Literature, grammar, composition
- **Hindi**: Literature, grammar, writing
- **Mathematics**: Algebra, geometry, data handling
- **Science**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (separate focus)
- **Social Science**: History, Geography, Civics, Economics
- **Sanskrit/Third Language**: Optional
