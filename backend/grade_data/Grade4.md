# Grade 4 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 4 is the final year of primary school. Students aged 9-10 years consolidate primary skills in preparation for middle school.

## Subjects
- **English**: Advanced grammar, composition, literature
- **Hindi**: Literature, advanced grammar, creative writing
- **Mathematics**: Large numbers, fractions, decimals introduction
- **EVS**: Detailed science and social studies concepts
- **Computer Science**: MS Office basics, internet safety
- **Art & Craft**: Advanced creative projects

## Syllabus Summary
- **English**: All tenses, voice, direct-indirect speech, essay writing
- **Hindi**: Formal/informal letters, story writing, grammar mastery
- **Mathematics**: 6-digit numbers, fractions, decimals, geometry, data handling
- **EVS**: Human body systems, Indian geography, history glimpses

## Teaching Method
- **Expert Teachers**: Subject specialists for each subject
- **Interactive Tools**: Digital whiteboards, simulations
- **Group Discussions**: Critical thinking development
- **Self-Study Skills**: Developing independent learning habits
- **Class Duration**: 5-6 hours daily
