# Grade 3 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 3 introduces students aged 8-9 years to more complex concepts. Focus shifts to analytical thinking and independent learning skills.

## Subjects
- **English**: Literature, grammar, creative writing, comprehension
- **Hindi**: Literature, grammar, essay writing
- **Mathematics**: 4-digit numbers, all operations, fractions introduction
- **EVS**: Science concepts, social awareness, environment
- **Computer Science**: Basic computer operations, typing
- **Art & Craft**: Creative projects

## Syllabus Summary
- **English**: Parts of speech, tenses, story writing, poetry appreciation
- **Hindi**: Unseen passages, essay writing, letter formats
- **Mathematics**: Numbers up to 9999, multiplication/division, fractions, time, money
- **EVS**: Water cycle, food chain, community helpers, maps introduction

## Teaching Method
- **Conceptual Learning**: Focus on understanding, not rote learning
- **Live Classes**: 5 hours daily with subject rotation
- **Homework Support**: Online homework help sessions
- **Reading Program**: Library access with reading challenges
- **Batch Size**: 20 students maximum
