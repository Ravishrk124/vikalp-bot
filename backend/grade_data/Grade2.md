# Grade 2 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 2 at Vikalp Online School builds upon Grade 1 foundations. Students aged 7-8 years develop stronger reading, writing, and mathematical skills.

## Subjects
- **English**: Reading comprehension, creative writing, grammar
- **Hindi**: Reading, writing, grammar, literature
- **Mathematics**: 3-digit numbers, multiplication concept, geometry
- **EVS**: Community, animals, plants, water conservation
- **Art Education**: Creative expression through art
- **Computer Basics**: Introduction to computers

## Syllabus Summary
- **English**: Story reading, paragraph writing, nouns, verbs, adjectives
- **Hindi**: Unseen passages, letter writing, grammar rules
- **Mathematics**: Numbers up to 999, addition/subtraction with carry, multiplication tables (2-5)
- **EVS**: Our neighborhood, types of houses, family tree, animals and their homes

## Teaching Method
- **Interactive Classes**: Daily live sessions with expert teachers
- **Reading Circle**: Group reading activities
- **Math Lab**: Virtual manipulatives for concepts
- **Project-Based Learning**: Monthly projects
- **Homework**: 30-45 minutes daily
- **Class Size**: 15-20 students per batch
