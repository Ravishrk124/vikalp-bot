# Grade 10 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 10 is the board examination year. Students aged 15-16 years prepare for CBSE/NIOS board exams with intensive coaching.

## FAQs
**Q: When are CBSE board exams held?**
A: February-March every year.

**Q: What is the difference between Standard and Basic Math?**
A: Basic Math for students not taking Math in 11-12. Standard for Science stream.

**Q: How is internal assessment calculated?**
A: 20 marks internal (periodic tests, notebook, practical) + 80 marks board exam.

**Q: Is there a crash course for board preparation?**
A: Yes, 3-month intensive crash course available from December.
