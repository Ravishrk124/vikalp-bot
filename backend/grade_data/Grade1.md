# Grade 1 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 1 marks the beginning of formal primary education at Vikalp Online School. Children aged 6-7 years develop fundamental academic skills with CBSE-aligned curriculum.

## Subjects
- **English**: Reading, writing, grammar basics, comprehension
- **Hindi**: Reading, writing, grammar, poems
- **Mathematics**: Numbers, operations, shapes, measurement
- **EVS (Environmental Studies)**: Combined Science & Social Studies
- **Art Education**: Drawing, painting, creative activities
- **Physical Education**: Indoor exercises, yoga basics

## Syllabus Summary
- **English**: Alphabets revision, three-letter words, sentences, basic grammar, story reading
- **Hindi**: Varnamala, matras, simple passages, poems
- **Mathematics**: Numbers 1-100, place value, addition/subtraction, shapes, patterns
- **EVS**: My body, family, plants, animals, water, food, shelter

## Teaching Method
- **Live Interactive Classes**: 4-5 hours daily with breaks
- **Doubt Clearing Sessions**: Weekly sessions
- **Digital Content**: NCERT-based animated lessons
- **Practice & Homework**: Daily assignments
- **Parent Updates**: Weekly progress emails
- **Batch Size**: 15-20 students

## FAQs
**Q: Is the curriculum CBSE or NIOS?**
A: Both options available. Parents can choose during admission.

**Q: What are class timings?**
A: Morning: 9 AM - 1 PM or Afternoon: 2 PM - 6 PM (with breaks).

**Q: How are classes conducted?**
A: Live online classes via Zoom/Google Meet with recorded backup.

**Q: Are notebooks and textbooks provided?**
A: NCERT textbooks and workbooks available for purchase.
