# Grade 11 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 11 begins senior secondary education with stream selection. Students aged 16-17 years choose Science, Commerce, or Humanities.

## Stream Options

### Science Stream
- Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
- English Core
- Optional: Computer Science/Physical Education

### Commerce Stream  
- Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics
- English Core
- Optional: Mathematics/Informatics Practices

### Humanities Stream
- History, Political Science, Geography/Psychology
- English Core
- Optional: Economics/Sociology

## Syllabus Summary
- **Science**: Motion, thermodynamics, organic chemistry, cell biology
- **Commerce**: Accounting principles, business environment, microeconomics
- **Humanities**: Indian history, political theory, human geography

## Teaching Method
- **Stream-Specific Focus**: Specialized coaching per stream
- **Competitive Exam Prep**: JEE/NEET foundation (Science)
- **Practical Labs**: Virtual and home-kit experiments
- **Career Guidance**: Regular counseling sessions
- **Study Hours**: 8 hours daily

## Fee Plan
- **Admission Fee**: ₹15,000 (one-time)

- **Annual Package**: ₹80,000/year (2 months free)
- **Books & Lab Materials**: ₹6,000/year

## FAQs
**Q: Can I change stream after Grade 11?**
A: Not recommended, but possible with conditions.

**Q: Is JEE/NEET coaching separate?**
A: Basic preparation integrated; advanced coaching available separately.

**Q: What are the practical requirements?**
A: Virtual labs + home practical kits for Science students.

**Q: How are internal marks calculated?**
A: 20 marks internal assessment, 80 marks theory exam.
