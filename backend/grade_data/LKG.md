# LKG (Lower Kindergarten) - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
LKG at Vikalp Online School is designed for children aged 4-5 years. We build upon Nursery foundations with more structured learning while maintaining a play-based approach.

## Subjects
- **English**: Alphabets, phonics, three-letter words, simple sentences
- **Hindi**: Hindi varnamala, matras introduction, simple words
- **Mathematics**: Numbers 1-50, addition concept, shapes, patterns
- **EVS**: Family, home, community helpers, seasons, festivals
- **Art & Craft**: Advanced drawing, origami, craft projects
- **Rhymes & Stories**: Action rhymes, picture story reading

## Syllabus Summary
- English alphabet writing with phonics mastery
- Hindi alphabet writing and basic matras
- Numbers 1-50 recognition and writing
- Simple addition (numbers up to 10)
- Shape patterns and sequences
- Days of the week, months introduction
- Community helpers and their roles
- Seasons and festivals of India

## Teaching Method
- **Interactive Sessions**: Live classes with teacher interaction
- **Activity-Based Learning**: Hands-on activities for each concept
- **Small Groups**: 10-12 students per batch
- **Multimedia Content**: Educational videos and songs
- **Practice Worksheets**: Daily practice sheets for parents
- **Session Duration**: 45-minute classes

## FAQs
**Q: What is the difference between LKG and Nursery?**
A: LKG has more structured curriculum with writing practice and concept building.

**Q: What board curriculum is followed?**
A: CBSE-aligned curriculum for seamless transition.

**Q: How is the child's progress tracked?**
A: Through observation, activity completion, and monthly reports.
