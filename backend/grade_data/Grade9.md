# Grade 9 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 9 begins secondary school education. Students aged 14-15 years start board exam preparation with CBSE/NIOS curriculum.

## Subjects
- **English**: Language & Literature (CBSE)
- **Hindi**: Course A/B options
- **Mathematics**: Advanced algebra, geometry, statistics
- **Science**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (combined)
- **Social Science**: History, Geography, Political Science, Economics
- **Computer Applications/IT**: Optional 6th subject

## Syllabus Summary
- **Mathematics**: Number systems, polynomials, coordinate geometry, triangles, statistics
- **Science**: Motion, force, atoms, tissues, natural resources
- **Social Science**: French Revolution, India geography, democracy, development
- **English**: Beehive, Moments (NCERT textbooks)

## Teaching Method
- **Board Pattern**: Complete alignment with CBSE/NIOS
- **Intensive Practice**: Daily problem-solving
- **Sample Papers**: Weekly practice tests
- **Doubt Sessions**: One-on-one support
- **Study Hours**: 7-8 hours daily

## FAQs
**Q: Which board is better - CBSE or NIOS?**
A: CBSE for regular schooling, NIOS for flexibility. Both equally recognized.

**Q: Is Grade 9 important for boards?**
A: Yes, 80% syllabus connects to Grade 10 boards.

**Q: What is the difference between Hindi Course A and B?**
A: Course A is for Hindi medium, Course B for English medium students.

**Q: Are there extra classes for Science?**
A: Yes, additional doubt-clearing and practice sessions.
