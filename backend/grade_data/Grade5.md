# Grade 5 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 5 marks the beginning of middle school at Vikalp. Students aged 10-11 years experience subject separation and deeper conceptual learning.

## Subjects
- **English**: Literature, advanced grammar, writing skills
- **Hindi**: Literature, composition, advanced grammar
- **Mathematics**: Decimals, fractions, geometry, data handling
- **Science**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology basics
- **Social Studies**: History, Geography, Civics
- **Computer Science**: Programming introduction (Scratch)

## Syllabus Summary
- **English**: Creative writing, comprehension strategies, literary devices
- **Hindi**: Prose, poetry, formal communication
- **Mathematics**: LCM/HCF, fractions operations, percentage introduction, geometry
- **Science**: Living organisms, materials, motion, light
- **Social Studies**: Ancient India, Indian geography, democratic governance

## Teaching Method
- **Subject Experts**: Specialized teachers for each subject
- **Laboratory Sessions**: Virtual science experiments
- **Map Work**: Geography practical sessions
- **Critical Thinking**: Debate and discussion sessions
- **Study Hours**: 6 hours daily with breaks

## FAQs
**Q: When do Science and Social Studies start separately?**
A: From Grade 5, EVS is split into Science and Social Studies.

**Q: Is there any choice in subjects?**
A: All subjects are mandatory in Grade 5.

**Q: What programming language is taught?**
A: Visual programming with Scratch to build logic.

**Q: Are Olympiads encouraged?**
A: Yes, IMO, NSO, and other Olympiad preparations available.
