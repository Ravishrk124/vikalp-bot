# Grade 7 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 7 continues middle school education with increasing complexity. Students aged 12-13 years develop analytical and reasoning skills.

## Subjects
- **English**: Literature, advanced grammar, writing skills
- **Hindi**: Prose, poetry, grammar
- **Mathematics**: Algebra, geometry, data handling
- **Science**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
- **Social Science**: History, Geography, Civics
- **Sanskrit/Third Language**: Optional
- **Computer Science**: Python introduction

## Syllabus Summary
- **Mathematics**: Rational numbers, linear equations, triangles, data handling, mensuration
- **Science**: Nutrition, respiration, heat, light, motion, acids-bases
- **Social Science**: Medieval India, natural resources, democratic processes
- **English**: NCERT Honeycomb, supplementary reader

## Teaching Method
- **Interactive Learning**: Discussion-based classes
- **Problem Solving**: Focus on application
- **Lab Sessions**: Virtual experiments
- **Career Awareness**: Introduction to career paths
- **Class Hours**: 6-7 hours daily
