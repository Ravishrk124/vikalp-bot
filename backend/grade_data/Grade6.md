# Grade 6 - Vikalp Online School (CBSE/NIOS)

## Overview
Grade 6 at Vikalp deepens subject knowledge with NCERT curriculum. Students aged 11-12 years develop strong academic foundations.

## Subjects
- **English**: Literature, grammar, writing (NCERT)
- **Hindi**: Literature, grammar, composition
- **Mathematics**: Number system, algebra basics, geometry
- **Science**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
- **Social Science**: History, Geography, Civics
- **Sanskrit/Third Language**: Optional
- **Computer Science**: Coding basics

## Syllabus Summary
- **Mathematics**: Integers, fractions, algebra introduction, ratio, geometry, symmetry
- **Science**: Food, materials, living world, motion, light, electricity
- **Social Science**: Early humans, Indian history, Earth geography, democracy
- **English**: NCERT textbooks - Honeysuckle, A Pact with the Sun

## Teaching Method
- **NCERT-Based**: Complete alignment with CBSE curriculum
- **Conceptual Videos**: Pre-class flipped learning
- **Practice Sessions**: Extra problem-solving classes
- **Doubt Sessions**: Daily doubt clearing
- **Study Duration**: 6-7 hours daily
