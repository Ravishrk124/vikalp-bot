# Indian Languages - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Learn Indian Languages through Vikalp's comprehensive online courses. Connect with diverse communities and explore the rich tapestry of Indian culture through language learning.

## Available Languages
- **Hindi** - National language, essential for pan-India communication
- **Tamil** - Classical Dravidian language spoken in Tamil Nadu
- **Telugu** - Widely spoken in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana
- **Kannada** - Official language of Karnataka
- **Malayalam** - Spoken in Kerala and Lakshadweep
- **Marathi** - Official language of Maharashtra
- **Bengali** - Spoken in West Bengal and Bangladesh
- **Gujarati** - Language of Gujarat
- **Sanskrit** - Ancient classical language of India
- **Urdu** - Rich literary tradition, widely understood
- **Arabic** - Important for religious and cultural studies
