# Classical Music - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Music Classes for Kids is a 9-month course exploring the world of Indian classical music. From vocal training to music theory, cultivate your passion for music and develop your skills.

## Available Music Courses
### Hindustani Classical Singing
- Origin: North Indian classical tradition
- Raag-based melodic framework
- Taal (rhythmic cycles)
- Bandish and composition singing
- Suitable for all ages

### Carnatic Music
- Origin: South Indian classical tradition
- Varnam and Kriti compositions
- Swara exercises
- Alapana and improvisation
- Rich tradition of devotional music

## Course Structure
- **Duration**: 9 months
- **Classes per week**: 2 sessions
- **Session length**: 45-60 minutes
- **Platform**: Zoom with audio optimization
- **Batch size**: 6-8 students (or 1:1 available)

## Curriculum Includes
- Voice training and breathing exercises
- Sargam (Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni Sa)
- Alankar (melodic patterns)
- Taal knowledge (rhythm)
- Simple ragas/raagams
- Compositions and songs
- Music theory basics

## FAQs
**Q: What age can children start?**
A: Children from age 5 can begin learning music.

**Q: Do you teach instruments?**
A: This course focuses on vocal training. Instrument classes are under Hobby Classes.

**Q: Are exams conducted?**
A: Yes, students can appear for graded exams (Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, etc.).

**Q: Can adults join?**
A: Yes, separate batches available for adults.
