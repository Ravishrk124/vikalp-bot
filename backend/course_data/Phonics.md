# Phonics for Kids - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Phonics for Kids is a 9-month course focused on speech and pronunciation. Children learn the sounds that each letter makes and how letter order affects word meaning. This systematic approach helps children decode written language effectively.

## Why Phonics is Important
- Children learn letter-sound relationships
- Helps read unfamiliar words
- Develops spelling skills
- Foundation for fluent reading
- Better pronunciation and speech clarity

## Phonics Approach vs Whole Language
### Whole Language (Traditional)
- Rote memorization of words
- Visual memory based
- Words memorized as separate units

### Phonics Approach (Our Method)
- Analytical skills for decoding
- Pattern recognition
- Sound blending techniques
- Applies to unfamiliar words

- Letter sounds (phonemes)
- Blending sounds

## FAQs
**Q: What is the best age to start?**
A: Children from age 3 can start phonics learning.

**Q: Is this for reading or speaking?**
A: Both! Phonics helps with reading, spelling, and pronunciation.

**Q: How can parents support?**
A: Parents can reinforce learning through daily reading and sound practice at home.

## Parent Involvement
Parents play a crucial role in fostering children's reading development:
- Recycle learned words in daily conversations
- Read stories encountered in class together
- Ask about beginning and ending sounds
- Practice letter-sound relationships daily
