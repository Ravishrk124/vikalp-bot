# Yoga for Kids - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Yoga for Kids is a 9-month course promoting health and wellness through engaging activities and mindful practices. Nurture young minds and bodies with ancient Indian wellness traditions.

## Course Details
- **Duration**: 9 months
- **Classes per week**: 2-3 sessions
- **Session length**: 30-45 minutes
- **Platform**: Zoom with video-on
- **Batch size**: 10-12 students
- **Age group**: 4-16 years (age-appropriate batches)

## Curriculum Includes
### Physical Practices
- Basic yoga asanas (postures)
- Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)
- Stretching exercises
- Balance poses
- Flexibility training

### Breathing & Mindfulness
- Pranayama (breathing exercises)
- Meditation basics
- Relaxation techniques
- Mindfulness activities
- Stress management

### Theory & Values
- Yoga philosophy basics
- Healthy lifestyle habits
- Importance of nutrition
- Daily routine (Dinacharya)
