# Public Speaking for Kids - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Public Speaking for Kids is an online course for school-going children who want to communicate effectively and become leaders. The course builds confidence, presentation skills, and effective communication.

## Course Levels
### Beginners Level
- **Age**: 3-6 years
- Focus: Basic expression, confidence building
- Simple storytelling and show-and-tell
- Duration: 3 months
- **Fee**: ₹15,900

### Intermediate Level
- **Age**: 7-10 years
- Focus: Presentation skills, vocabulary building
- Group discussions and debates
- Duration: 3 months

### Advanced Level
- **Age**: 11-13 years
- Focus: Persuasive speaking, critical thinking
- Mock interviews and speeches
- Duration: 3 months

### Professional Level
- **Age**: 14-16 years
- Focus: Leadership communication, business presentations
- Public debate and Model UN style activities
- Duration: 3 months
- **Fee**: ₹66,000 (full level)

## Unique Teaching Approach
Each level includes classes by 4 types of professionals:
1. **Film & Theater Professionals** - Art of expression and storytelling
2. **Journalists** - Presentation skills and articulation
3. **Entrepreneurs** - Logical thinking and creativity
4. **English & Phonics Experts** - Correct language, speech, pronunciation

## Course Structure
- **Duration**: 3 months per level
- **Classes per week**: 2-3 sessions
- **Platform**: Zoom
- **Batch size**: 8-10 students

## Fee Structure
- **Per Level**: ₹15,900 - ₹20,000
- **Full Course (4 levels)**: ₹50,000 - ₹66,000
- **Demo Class**: Free trial available

## FAQs
**Q: Is this only for shy children?**
A: No, all children benefit from communication skills training.

**Q: What is the class format?**
A: Interactive activities, practice sessions, group discussions, and individual presentations.

**Q: Are there competitions?**
A: Yes, inter-batch competitions and external participation opportunities.
