# Hobby Classes - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Hobby Classes for Kids is a 9-month course offering a variety of classes to help children pursue their interests and unleash their creativity. From music to art, explore your passion!

## Available Hobby Classes

### Musical Instruments
- **Online Guitar Classes** - Acoustic and classical guitar
- **Online Piano Classes** - Keyboard and piano basics to advanced
- **Online Drum Classes** - Rhythm and percussion training

### Dance
- **Hip Hop Dance** - Modern street dance styles

### Art & Creativity
- **Digital Painting** - Using tablets and digital tools
- **Sketching** - Pencil drawing techniques
- **Digital Photography** - Camera basics and composition

### Mindfulness
- **Meditation & Mindfulness** - Mental wellness for children

### Games
- **Chess for Kids** - Strategy and critical thinking

## Course Structure
- **Duration**: 9 months per hobby
- **Classes per week**: 1-2 sessions
- **Session length**: 45-60 minutes
- **Platform**: Zoom
- **Batch size**: 6-10 students

## Fee Structure (varies by hobby)
- **Guitar/Piano/Drums**: ₹3,500 - ₹5,000/month

## FAQs
**Q: What age groups are these for?**
A: Most hobby classes are for ages 6 and above. Specific age requirements vary.

**Q: Do I need to buy instruments?**
A: Yes, for music classes you'll need the respective instrument. We provide buying guidance.

**Q: Can I try multiple hobbies?**
A: Yes, you can enroll in multiple hobby classes.

**Q: Are there performances/exhibitions?**
A: Yes, virtual showcases and recitals organized periodically.
