# Classical Dance - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Dance Classes for Kids is a 9-month course where young dancers can explore Indian classical dance forms in a supportive online environment. Nurture your child's passion for dance and creativity.

## Available Dance Forms
### Bharatanatyam
- Origin: Tamil Nadu
- Oldest classical dance form
- Combines expression, rhythm, and drama
- Traditional temple dance style

### Kathak
- Origin: North India
- Storytelling through dance
- Intricate footwork and spins
- Expressive hand gestures

## Course Structure
- **Duration**: 9 months
- **Classes per week**: 2 sessions
- **Session length**: 1 hour
- **Platform**: Zoom with video-on
- **Batch size**: 8-10 students

## Curriculum Includes
- Basic postures (Araimandi/Samabhanga)
- Hand gestures (Mudras)
- Footwork patterns (Adavus/Tatkar)
- Rhythmic patterns (Taal)
- Expressions (Abhinaya)
- Simple compositions
- Dance history and theory
