# Foreign Languages - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Embark on a journey to learn foreign languages with Vikalp India. Master Spanish, French, or German with our structured courses designed for school-going children and adults.

## Available Languages
- **Spanish** - 4 levels (Beginner to Professional)
- **French** - 4 levels (Beginner to Professional)
- **German** - 4 levels (Beginner to Professional)

## Course Levels
### Beginners Level
- For complete beginners
- Introduction to alphabets, basic vocabulary, greetings
- Simple sentence formation
- Duration: 3 months

### Intermediate Level
- For students with basic knowledge
- Advanced vocabulary, grammar rules
- Reading comprehension
- Duration: 3 months

### Advanced Level
- Complex grammar and tenses
- Essay writing, formal communication
- Duration: 3 months

### Professional Level
- Business communication
- Exam preparation (DELE, DELF, Goethe)
- Certification support
- Duration: 3 months

## Course Structure
- **Duration**: 3 months per level (Full course: 12 months)
- **Classes per week**: 3 sessions
- **Platform**: Zoom/Google Meet
- **Batch size**: 6-10 students

**Q: Are learning materials provided?**
