# Computer Science - Vikalp Online School

## Overview
Discover computer courses at Vikalp India, offering engaging and educational programs to empower young learners with essential tech skills. Prepare your child for success in the digital age.

## Available Courses
### Computer Science for Beginners
- Age: 5-7 years
- Introduction to computers
- Basic operations (mouse, keyboard)
- Paint and drawing applications
- Duration: 6 months

### Computer Science for Primary
- Grades: 1-5
- MS Office basics (Word, PowerPoint)
- Internet safety and usage
- Basic typing skills
- Introduction to multimedia
- Duration: Academic year

### Computer Science for Middle School
- Grades: 6-8
- Advanced MS Office
- Introduction to programming concepts
- Spreadsheet applications
- Digital citizenship
- Duration: Academic year

### Computer Science for High School
- Grades: 9-12
- Programming languages (Python, Java)
- Web development
- Database management
- Computer networks basics
- Board exam preparation (if applicable)
- Duration: Academic year
